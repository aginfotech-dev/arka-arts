FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a branding project take?
+
A complete brand identity typically takes 7–14 business days. Timelines vary by scope - a logo alone can be done in 3–5 days, while a full brand system with guidelines may take 3 weeks. We always share a project timeline before starting.
Do you redesign existing logos?
+
Absolutely. We offer both full rebrands and logo refinements. We start with a brand audit to understand what's working, what's not, and what your customers currently associate with your visual identity.
Can you work with international clients?
+
Yes - we work with clients across 22+ countries. All communication happens via email, WhatsApp, or video call. We're fluent in English and comfortable with international business culture and timezones.
Do you provide source files?
+
Yes. All final deliverables include full source files - AI, EPS, SVG, PNG, PDF - so you own your assets completely. You're never locked into working with us.
How many revisions are included?
+
Our standard packages include 3 rounds of revisions. We find this is more than enough for most projects because we invest heavily in the discovery phase to get the brief right before we start designing.
